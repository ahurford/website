Dr. Amy Hurford

I am an Associate Professor jointly appointed in the Department of Biology and the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Memorial University of Newfoundland.

My research is in theoretical ecology, evolution and epidemiology. Specifically, my research considers:

  1. Public health responses to COVID-19 in Atlantic Canada;

  2. Population dynamics with temperature dependence and explicit space; and

  3. Evolutionary epidemiology.
